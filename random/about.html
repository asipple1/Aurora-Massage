Welcome to Aurora Massage Therapy!

At Aurora Massage Therapy, it is our mission to provide a stress-free environment where you can rest, relax and restore your body, mind and soul.

  

Through state certification in Nebraska, over 800 hours in the classroom and thousands of hours of hands on experience, Susan is able to provide her clients with an abundance of knowledge and the massage to best suit your needs.

Susan is well versed in various techniques and she offers a number of different massages such as deep tissue, Swedish, trigger point, myofascial release, shiatsu, and hot stones.  In addition to the aforementioned techniques, Susan is also certified in prenatal/pregnancy and postpartum massage.
