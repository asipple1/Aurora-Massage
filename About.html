At Aurora Massage Therapy, it is our mission to provide a retreat where you can rest, relax and rejuvenate your body, mind and soul. Through state certification in Nebraska, over 1200 hours in the classroom and thousands of hours of hands on experience, Susan is able to provide her clients with an abundance of knowledge and the massage to best suit your needs. Susan is well versed in various techniques and she offers a variety of massages such as Deep Tissue, Swedish, Trigger Point, Myofascial Release, Shiatsu and Hot Stones. In addition to the aforementioned techniques, Susan is certified in prenatal/pregnancy and postpartum massage.
